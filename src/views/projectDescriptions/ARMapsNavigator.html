AR Maps Navigator

An AR navigation tool that allows for real-time projected guidance through your phone camera to take you from point A to point B.

Project Overview

This project was meant to be a cool proof of concept of AR based navigation tools that they could be used as a way of more intuitive navigation as our world infrastructure, especially in cities, becomes more and more complex.

It utilizes the ARcore SDK for android to provide your android device with AR feature sets such as environmental position tracking and plane detection. From there, I can then use the Google Maps API to pull in navigation data for general directional input and then calculate pathing checkpoints to place on the floor of the AR space to get from source to destination

The pathing checkpoints are determined by segmentation vision models that can identify practical walking surfaces in the camera view and then place the checkpoints on those surfaces to ensure that the projected path is always walkable.
